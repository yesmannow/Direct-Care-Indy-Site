# DPC Launch Strategy - Non-Negotiables

## Overview
Direct Primary Care (DPC) launch targeting Indianapolis metro area with a 90/10 care model. Focus on transparent pricing, specialist-led primary care, and insurance-free healthcare delivery.

## Core Non-Negotiables

### Target Metrics
- **Panel Size**: 500 members per provider (sustainable clinical load)
- **Age Range**: 13+ years (family-inclusive, no upper age limit)
- **Geography**: Indianapolis metro area (initial focus on NW Indianapolis)

### Target Segments (Priority Order)
1. **Small Business Employees** (≤50 employees) - Primary target
2. **Self-Employed Professionals** - High-value individuals
3. **Families with Children** - Family planning and pediatric care
4. **Young Professionals** (25-45) - Preventive care focus

### Brand Identity
- **Tagline**: "Specialist-Led Primary Care Without Insurance Hassles"
- **Value Proposition**: "90% of your healthcare covered for one flat monthly fee"
- **Communication Principle**: Show value and care quality BEFORE asking for commitment

### "Not Insurance" Language (Critical)
**Approved Language:**
- "This is not insurance. This is direct primary care."
- "We handle 90% of your healthcare needs. Insurance covers the 10%."
- "No copays, no deductibles, no surprise bills."

**Prohibited Language:**
- "Insurance alternative"
- "Better than insurance"
- "Insurance replacement"

### Service Scope
**Included Services (90% Model):**
- Unlimited sick visits and chronic disease management
- Preventive care and wellness exams
- Basic labs (CBC, CMP, A1C, Lipid Panel) at wholesale prices
- Immunizations and injections
- Minor procedures (stitches, cryotherapy, skin biopsies)
- Pharmacy access at wholesale prices
- 24/7 text-based communication with PA
- Email/phone access to Dr. Pike for complex cases

**Excluded Services (10% - Insurance Domain):**
- Hospitalizations
- Surgeries
- Specialist referrals
- Emergency care
- Ambulance services
- Durable medical equipment
- Advanced imaging (MRI/CT)

### Pricing Structure
**Individual Tiers:**
- Single: $99/month
- Couple: $149/month
- Family: $199/month

**Business Plans:**
- Per employee: $69/month (minimum 3 employees)
- Volume discounts for 10+ employees

### HSA/FSA Compatibility
- **Approved**: HSA-eligible for individuals 55+
- **Approved**: FSA-eligible for businesses
- **Communication**: "HSA-eligible for qualifying individuals"

### Age Restrictions & Policies
- **Minimum Age**: 13 years old
- **No Upper Age Limit**: Open to seniors
- **Family Coverage**: All family members welcome
- **No Pre-Existing Condition Exclusions**: Pure direct care model

### Round Table Model (Differentiation)
- **PA-Led Daily Care**: High-access nurse practitioners
- **Weekly Specialist Oversight**: Dr. Pike reviews complex cases
- **90/10 Split**: Routine care (90%) vs. complex care (10%)
- **Communication**: "Your PA handles 90%, Dr. Pike covers 10%"

### Marketing Principles
1. **Lead with Value**: Show care quality and transparency first
2. **Educate Before Selling**: Explain DPC model thoroughly
3. **Trust Building**: Highlight specialist credentials and experience
4. **Local Focus**: Emphasize Indianapolis community connection
5. **Family-Centered**: Position as comprehensive family healthcare

### Risk Management
- **Clinical Oversight**: All care reviewed by board-certified physician
- **Documentation**: Complete medical records maintained
- **Emergency Protocols**: Clear guidance on when to seek emergency care
- **Transition Support**: Help patients understand insurance's role

### Compliance Requirements
- **HIPAA Compliance**: Full patient privacy protection
- **Medical Records**: Complete documentation for each visit
- **Prescription Tracking**: Pharmacy records maintained
- **Quality Metrics**: Track patient outcomes and satisfaction

## Implementation Timeline
- **Phase 1**: Build stabilization and content foundation ✅
- **Phase 2**: Content model and information architecture (Current)
- **Phase 3**: Visual design and conversion optimization
- **Phase 4**: Launch and enrollment (500 member target)

## Success Metrics
- **Enrollment**: 500 members within 12 months
- **Retention**: 85% annual retention rate
- **Satisfaction**: 4.5+ star rating
- **Utilization**: Appropriate care-seeking patterns
- **Cost Savings**: Demonstrable value vs. traditional insurance
