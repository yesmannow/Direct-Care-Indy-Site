# DPC Site Map - Information Architecture

## Overview
Clean, conversion-focused information architecture that communicates DPC value proposition and guides users through enrollment funnel.

## Core Pages (Conversion-Focused)

### 1. Home (/)
**Purpose**: DPC overview + primary enrollment CTA
**Key Content**:
- Hero: "Specialist-Led Primary Care Without Insurance Hassles"
- 90/10 Model explanation
- Value propositions (no copays, transparent pricing, specialist access)
- Primary CTA: "Join Now - See Pricing"
- Social proof: Dr. Pike credentials, patient testimonials
- Trust indicators: HIPAA compliant, Indianapolis local

### 2. Membership (/membership)
**Purpose**: Pricing tiers and what's included
**Key Content**:
- Pricing table (Individual $99, Couple $149, Family $199)
- Business pricing (≤50 employees, $69/employee)
- What's included checklist (90% services)
- "Not insurance" disclaimer
- HSA/FSA eligibility information
- Enrollment process overview

### 3. How It Works (/how-it-works)
**Purpose**: Explain the 90/10 model and "not insurance" concept
**Key Content**:
- 90/10 model visualization
- "This is not insurance" explanation
- Round Table model (PA daily care + Dr. Pike oversight)
- Insurance's role in the 10%
- Age 13+ policy
- Indianapolis focus

### 4. Services Included (/services)
**Purpose**: Scope transparency and add-on services
**Key Content**:
- Included services checklist (90% model)
- Wholesale pricing examples (labs, pharmacy)
- Excluded services (10% - insurance domain)
- Specialist access explanation
- 24/7 communication features

### 5. For Employers (/employers)
**Purpose**: ≤50 employee business solutions
**Key Content**:
- Small business health benefits
- Per-employee pricing ($69/month)
- Employee enrollment process
- Business value proposition
- Partnership opportunities

## Support Pages (Trust-Building)

### 9. Locations (/locations)

### User Journey Flow
1. **Awareness** → Home (value proposition)
2. **Interest** → How It Works (education)
3. **Consideration** → Membership (pricing) + Services (transparency)
4. **Decision** → Join (enrollment)
5. **Retention** → FAQ + Resources (ongoing support)

## Content Strategy Principles

### Communication Hierarchy
1. **Lead with Value**: Care quality before pricing
2. **Educate First**: Explain DPC model thoroughly
3. **Trust Building**: Credentials and transparency
4. **Local Connection**: Indianapolis community focus
5. **Family-Centered**: Inclusive healthcare approach

### Conversion Optimization
- Clear value propositions on every page
- Objection-handling in FAQ
- Social proof throughout
- Transparent pricing always visible
- Multiple enrollment touchpoints

### SEO Strategy
- Primary keywords: "direct primary care Indianapolis"
- Long-tail: "DPC without insurance Indianapolis"
- Local modifiers: "NW Indianapolis primary care"
- Educational content for organic traffic
